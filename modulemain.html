About the Conference

Since the very first FisayoConf back in 2024, we have been guided by one principle: build the web with care. This conference brings together designers, developers, and creators who believe the web should work for everyone.

We cover HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript — the three core technologies of the modern web. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned developer, there is something here for you.

Don't miss our excellent speakers sharing cutting-edge techniques and real-world experience.
